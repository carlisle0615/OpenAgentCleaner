# Code of Conduct

This project aims to be straightforward, respectful, and technically rigorous.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and evidence.
- Assume good intent, but be precise about risks and failures.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project and its users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or exclusionary conduct.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or reviews.
- Sharing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and discussions that violate this policy. Repeated or severe violations may result in a ban from project spaces.
